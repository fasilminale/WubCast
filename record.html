Most users start recordings from the toolbar popup — it remembers your camera, mic and system audio preferences. This page is the full setup surface for when you want finer control. When the picker appears, select the screen, window or tab you want to capture.

🔊 System audio:

To record tab / system audio, tick "Share tab audio" (or "Share system audio") in the picker. Enabling system audio in the picker may disable microphone recording for that capture — use Enable microphone below if you want voice instead.
